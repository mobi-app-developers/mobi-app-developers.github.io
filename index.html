OUR PROBLEM STATEMENT

People moving long distances with personal means in some cases have space in there means of transport and find it wasteful moving with a free seat/s, so they try to pick passengers along the way heading to the same root to utilize the empty seat while earning money Upon this ground our group has agreed to develop a mobile app which connects a person travelling to any interested person taking the same route. In case an interested party accepts a request to join the journey, a notification is sent to the person that has initiated the request. A message platform will be created to provide a form of communication between the two parties where they will be able to come to an agreement

The vehicle match mobile application is an application that connects two users, one who owns a vehicle and the other a passenger.

App objectives

To connect the passenger with the appropriate driver that is taking the same route. To provide ease access to a specific means of transport to a person travelling long distances To enable a passenger to chat with a vehicle owner to confirm and conclude on the travel arrangements.
